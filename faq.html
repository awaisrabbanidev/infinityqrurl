Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about our QR code generator and URL shortener services

QR Code Generator FAQs

Creating QR Codes

How do I create a QR code?

It's simple! Use the free QR code generator. Select the type (e.g., URL, vCard), enter your information, and generate QR code. You can then download the QR code image as a PNG.

What is a dynamic QR code?

A dynamic QR code allows you to change the destination URL without changing the QR code itself. This is perfect for campaigns where the link might need updating, and it's a feature of our dynamic QR code generator.

Can I make a custom QR code with my logo?

Absolutely. The QR code generator with logo feature lets you create a QR code with logo in the center, enhancing brand recognition. You can also adjust colors for a white QR code or colorful QR design.

QR Code Usage

What types of QR codes can I create?

Our QR code generator supports various types including URL codes, vCard contact codes, WiFi network codes, text codes, email codes, and SMS codes. This versatility makes it suitable for multiple applications.

Are there limitations on QR code scans?

No, there are no limitations on the number of scans for your QR codes. Both static and dynamic QR codes can be scanned unlimited times without any restrictions.

Can I track QR code scans?

Yes, with our dynamic QR codes, you can track scan statistics including the number of scans, location data, device types, and timestamps. This provides valuable insights for your marketing campaigns.

URL Shortener FAQs

Getting Started

What is a URL shortener?

A URL shortener is a tool that takes a long URL and creates a shortened link. This is useful for saving space on platforms like Twitter and for creating clean-looking links for print.

Is this URL shortener free?

Yes, a robust free URL shortener plan is available. For advanced features like custom URL shortener links and detailed analytics, premium plans are offered.

How do I shorten a link?

Paste your long link into the URL shortener tool on the website and click "Shorten." You will instantly receive a new, shortened URL to share.

Advanced Features

Can I create custom short URLs?

Yes, our platform allows you to create custom aliases for your shortened URLs. This means you can choose a memorable, branded ending for your short links instead of random characters.

What analytics are available for shortened URLs?

Our URL shortener provides detailed analytics including click counts, geographic location of clicks, referral sources, device types, and timestamps. This data helps you understand your audience better.

Do shortened URLs expire?

No, shortened URLs created with our service do not expire. They will remain active and functional indefinitely, ensuring your links always work.
