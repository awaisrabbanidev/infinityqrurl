How to Use Our QR Code & URL Shortener Tools

Step-by-step guides to help you create custom QR codes and shorten URLs effectively

Creating a QR Code

QR Code Generator Tutorial

1

Choose Your Content

Navigate to the QR code creator. Select what you want to link to: a website URL, a PDF document, plain text, or a vCard for your business card.

2

Generate and Customize

Click "Generate QR Code". Use the customization options to create a custom QR code. You can add a logo, change the qr color, and select different qr code shapes.

3

Download and Share

Once satisfied, download your QR code as a high-resolution PNG. It's now ready to be printed on materials or shared digitally.

Pro Tips for QR Codes

  • Always test your QR code before distribution to ensure it scans correctly
  • For print materials, use high-resolution PNG files for better quality
  • Consider using a frame or text near your QR code to indicate its purpose
  • For marketing campaigns, use dynamic QR codes to track scan analytics
  • When adding a logo, ensure it doesn't cover more than 30% of the QR code area

Shortening a URL

URL Shortener Tutorial

1

Copy Your Link

Find the long URL you wish to shorten from your website, YouTube video, or landing page.

2

Paste and Shorten

Go to the URL shortener page. Paste the link into the field. For a branded url shortener experience, you can create a custom name for your link.

3

Copy and Use

Click "Shorten Link". Your new, compact shortened url is ready. Use this link in your social media posts, emails, or marketing campaigns to track performance.

Pro Tips for URL Shortening

  • Use custom aliases that are memorable and relevant to your content
  • Track your link performance through our analytics dashboard
  • Use UTM parameters with your original URLs for enhanced tracking
  • Create different shortened URLs for different marketing channels to track effectiveness
  • For important links, consider setting up a custom domain for brand consistency

Advanced Techniques

Combining QR Codes & Short URLs

1

Create a Short URL First

Start by shortening your long URL using our URL shortener. This creates a clean, trackable link that's perfect for QR codes.

2

Generate QR Code with Short URL

Use your newly created short URL as the destination for your QR code. This combines the benefits of both technologies.

3

Track and Analyze

Monitor both QR code scans and URL clicks through our comprehensive analytics dashboard to understand user engagement.

Benefits of This Approach

  • Clean, professional appearance for printed materials
  • Comprehensive tracking of both scan and click data
  • Ability to update destination without changing QR code (with dynamic QR codes)
  • Brand consistency with custom short URLs
  • Optimized for social sharing and digital marketing

Ready to Get Started?

Put these tutorials into practice and start creating professional QR codes and shortened URLs for your business or personal projects.
